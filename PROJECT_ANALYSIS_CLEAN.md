## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

### Core Concept

A sophisticated WhatsApp-based intelligent tunnel system that acts as a unified business automation platform, routing to different services and sub-services as needed. The system will:

- Live on WhatsApp as an integrated bot with tags/identifiers
- Analyze conversations across multiple business chat groups
- Automatically execute business processes based on detected patterns
- Maintain organized document storage and retrieval
- Track and manage tasks with follow-up mechanisms
- Generate professional business documents (invoices, quotations, letterheads)

### Technical Architecture

**Primary Tunnel:** OpenClaw  
**Core Capability:** Acts as a tunnel/router that executes services when called

**Key Features:**
- Visualization and scraping of multiple file formats: PDF, WORD (.docx), EXCEL, JPEG, PNG
- Integration across multiple WhatsApp groups/chats simultaneously
- Document organization in structured folder hierarchies
- Network/graph-based sequence for document relationship tracking and retrieval

### Chat Groups Under Analysis

1. **Company Main Group** - General business operations and coordination
2. **Company Side Group** - Procurement and supplier management  
3. **Company Project Group** - Specific project tracking (Tank manufacturing, equipment sourcing)
4. **Personal Chats** - Direct communication with business stakeholders
